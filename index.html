Simulating the 2019 MLB Season in R

David Yamin

Combining pre-season projected performance metrics for Major League hitters and pitchers with Bill James’ Pythagorean Formula for teams’ projected winning percentage enables us the simulate the upcoming 2019 baseball season in R as many times as we wish.

Accessing My R Code and Datasets

My R code used to generate the model described below and related plots and graphics is available for download here. The 2019 datasets containing Fangraphs’ hitter and pitcher pre-season projected performances are available for download here for hitters and here for pitchers.
